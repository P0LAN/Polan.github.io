Polan

polan is the only open source voice assistant that works on windows *android and linux later*

respects your privacy security

polan does not sell or store your voice into any database, you can take a look at polan's codebase,it doesnt send or ping any server that stores your voice

built in C#,flutter and python code_blocks

C# is the most stable and fast language we choose, we're going to use flutter and python for the incoming mobile version

build by the community, for the community

this project by a community that cares about competing with closed-source corporate software that cannot be trusted

contribute for better FOSS

you can contribute by writing code, reporting bugs, donating to any FOSS contribiouters , we unfortunatly dont accept any kind of currency
